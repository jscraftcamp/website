About Our Event

JSCraftCamp is a free, community-driven Open Space gathering where 80-120 JavaScript enthusiasts come together to learn and share. Beyond just JavaScript, we explore the entire ecosystem - from frameworks and tooling to accessibility, testing, ethics in tech, and everything in between.

Our Open Space format means there's no predetermined agenda - participants propose sessions each morning, creating a dynamic, relevant experience. Everyone is both a teacher and a student, sharing knowledge and learning from peers in an inclusive environment.

What makes JSCraftCamp special is its grassroots nature - we're a volunteer-run event with no corporate ownership. Your support makes it possible to keep the event free for all participants while providing a comfortable space with good food, drinks, and everything needed for two days of community learning.
